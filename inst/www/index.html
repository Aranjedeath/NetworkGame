What is robustness?

Robustness is 'the ability of a system to resist change without adapting its initial stable configuration.' Basically, it's the abilitiy of a system to keep doing what it's supposed to do, even if there is a problem.

Why is robustness important?

Imagine if shutting down a single airport because of bad weather brought air traffic throughout the country to a standstill. That would be catastrophic! Luckily, human engineered networks and the networks of living systems are robust
